# Common Mistakes – Interview Questions

---

## 1. What is the most common system design interview mistake?

### Expected Answer
Skipping requirement clarification and jumping straight into architecture.

---

## 2. Why is over-engineering risky in interviews?

### Expected Answer
It adds complexity without justification and suggests weak prioritization.

---

## 3. How should you recover from a wrong assumption?

### Expected Answer
State the correction, update impacted design parts, and proceed with clear reasoning.

---

## 4. Why do interviewers care about trade-offs?

### Expected Answer
System design is about constraints and decisions, not a single perfect architecture.

---

## 5. What should be in your final summary?

### Expected Answer
Requirements, key architecture choices, scaling strategy, failures, and trade-offs.
