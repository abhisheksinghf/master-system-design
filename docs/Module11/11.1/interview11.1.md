# Interview Approach – Interview Questions

---

## 1. What is the first step in a system design interview?

### Expected Answer
Clarify functional and non-functional requirements before proposing architecture.

---

## 2. Why are scale estimates important early?

### Expected Answer
They drive core design choices like database type, partitioning, and caching strategy.

---

## 3. What should come before deep component discussions?

### Expected Answer
A clear high-level architecture and API/data model.

---

## 4. What trade-offs should always be discussed?

### Expected Answer
Latency vs consistency, availability vs correctness, and complexity vs maintainability.

---

## 5. What are interviewers primarily judging?

### Expected Answer
Structured thinking, communication, trade-off quality, and failure-aware design.
