# Mock Interviews – Interview Questions

---

## 1. Why are mock interviews necessary?

### Expected Answer
They build communication speed, structure, and confidence under time pressure.

---

## 2. What should a strong practice rubric include?

### Expected Answer
Clarity, correctness, trade-offs, failure handling, and time management.

---

## 3. How often should mock design interviews be practiced?

### Expected Answer
At least weekly with structured feedback and improvement tracking.

---

## 4. Why rotate constraints in practice sessions?

### Expected Answer
Different constraints force different architectures and improve adaptability.

---

## 5. What is the biggest mock-interview anti-pattern?

### Expected Answer
Memorizing solutions without understanding assumptions and trade-offs.
